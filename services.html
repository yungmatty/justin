Legitimate Digital & Financial Account Types for Business Operations

Here are various account categories that legitimate businesses might establish through proper registration processes:

1. Cryptocurrency Exchange Accounts
Examples: Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, Coinex, Poloniex, Bitget, BingX
Use: Trading cryptocurrencies, business payments in crypto, investment portfolios

2. Financial Technology Apps
Examples: PayPal, Venmo, Cash App, Wise (TransferWise), Revolut, Wirex, Simple App, Redotpay, Genome
Use: Digital payments, international transfers, business transactions

3. E-commerce & Marketplace Accounts
Examples: Amazon Seller Central, eBay Store, Shopify, Etsy Shop, Alibaba
Use: Online retail businesses, dropshipping, artisan sales

4. Traditional Banking Accounts
Examples: Checking accounts, savings accounts, money market accounts
Use: Business operations, payroll, expense management

5. International/Offshore Banking Accounts
Examples: Accounts in jurisdictions like Singapore, Switzerland, Cayman Islands
Use: International business operations, tax planning (when legal), asset protection

6. Business Shipping & Logistics Accounts
Examples: FedEx, UPS, DHL business accounts, USPS Commercial Base
Use: E-commerce fulfillment, business shipping discounts

7. Social Media & Digital Presence Accounts
Examples: Instagram Business, Facebook Page, Twitter Professional, LinkedIn Company Page
Use: Marketing, customer engagement, brand building

8. Additional Business Account Types:
Payment Processor Accounts: Stripe, Square, Authorize.net, Brand New Spending virtual/physical cards (Physical Cards take 15-30 days to be delivered)
Cloud Service Accounts: AWS, Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure
Software Platform Accounts: Salesforce, QuickBooks, Mailchimp
Betting Accounts: Bet365, Stake.com
Advertising Accounts: Google Ads, Facebook Ads Manager, TikTok Ads
